# Quack Tower Defense: Strategic Duck Warfare

<Note>
  Quack Tower Defense is a competitive multiplayer tower defense mode where players strategically place towers, manage economy, and overwhelm opponents with waves of enemies.
</Note>

## Core Mechanics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tower Placement">
    Strategically position defensive towers on your map to defend against incoming waves while maximizing coverage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Income Management">
    Balance spending between tower upgrades and income-generating structures to build economic advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wave Defense">
    Survive increasingly difficult waves of enemies while maintaining your defenses and economy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Offensive Strategy">
    Spend accumulated resources to send powerful mobs to your opponent's side, overwhelming their defenses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Strategy & Gameplay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early Game Foundation">
    Focus on balanced tower placement and income generation to establish a strong economic base.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mid Game Pressure">
    Begin sending strategic mobs while upgrading key defensive towers to handle escalating threats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Late Game Domination">
    Execute coordinated attacks with powerful mobs while maintaining impenetrable defenses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adapt and Overcome">
    Read your opponent's strategy and adjust your approach dynamically to exploit weaknesses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
