## Equipment System

<Note>
  Each duck has **3 equipment slots**: 2 weapon slots (for melee and/or ranged weapons) and 1 protection slot (for shields or defensive items). Choose your loadout wisely!
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="sword" title="Weapon Slots (2 Slots)">
    Equip up to 2 weapons found in chests across the battlefield:

    **Melee Weapons:**
    - **Swords**: Balanced melee weapons with limited hits (e.g., 4 hits)
    - **Hammers**: High damage, slower attacks
    - **Daggers**: Fast attacks, lower damage
    - **Spears**: Extended reach for melee combat

    **Ranged Weapons:**
    - **Bows**: Long-range attacks with limited arrows
    - **Crossbows**: Powerful ranged shots
    - **Throwing Weapons**: Short-range projectiles
    - **Magic Staffs**: Special ranged abilities

    **Slot Strategy**: Mix melee and ranged for versatility, or double down on your preferred combat style.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Protection Slot (1 Slot)">
    Equip one defensive item with durability:
    - **Wooden Shield**: Basic protection, low durability
    - **Metal Shield**: Strong defense, medium durability
    - **Tower Shield**: Maximum protection, heavy
    - **Buckler**: Light, fast, low protection
    - **Magic Barriers**: Special defensive abilities
    - **Armor Pieces**: Alternative protection options
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="box" title="Durability & Stacking">
    Manage your equipment strategically:
    - All equipment has limited uses/hits
    - Find duplicate weapons to stack charges
    - Example: Find 3 swords with 4 hits each = 12 total hits
    - Choose when to use powerful items
    - Risk vs reward in equipment management
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="treasure-chest" title="Chest System">
    Discover chests throughout the arena:
    - Random weapon/shield drops
    - Rarity tiers (Common to Legendary)
    - Consumable items
    - Temporary power-ups
    - Strategic positioning around chest spawns
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Strategy & Gameplay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early Game: Survive & Grow">
    Start by defeating weak AI mobs to build your base stats. Avoid stronger players and focus on safe power gains.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mid Game: Equipment & Positioning">
    Secure weapons and shields from chests. Position yourself between mob spawns and chest locations for efficient growth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Late Game: Risk & Domination">
    With substantial power, choose between hunting players for massive score gains or playing safe to maintain your position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Master Strategy: Adaptive Play">
    Read the battlefield constantly. Adapt your strategy based on your power level, available equipment, and nearby threats.
  </Step>
</Steps>
