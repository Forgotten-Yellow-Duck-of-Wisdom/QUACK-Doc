# Quack Ball: Where Ducks Clash

<Note>
  Inspired by Head Soccer, Quack Ball is an intense 1v1 game mode where ducks compete in fast-paced, minute-long matches to prove their superiority.
</Note>

## Strategy & Gameplay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Your Duck">
    Equip items and skills that complement your playstyle and duck's characteristics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Loadout">
    Select consumables for the match - once the game starts, equipment can't be changed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Master the Basics">
    Learn to predict ball trajectories and maintain game rhythm for consistent performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute Your Strategy">
    Combine skills, consumables, and timing to outmaneuver your opponent and score goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
