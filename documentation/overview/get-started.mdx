# Early Access to the Quack Universe

<Note>
  Quack is currently in early development. Our first game will launch in testnet, allowing us to fine-tune the experience while rewarding our early supporters.
</Note>

## Join the Alpha

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Premium Ring">
    Purchase a Quack Lord or Quack Emperor ring from OpenSea to secure your place in the alpha.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn exclusive rewards">
    Early detentor will benefits from special launch rewards (allow list, game items, token airdrops, etc...).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare for Launch">
    Join our community channels to stay updated on the latest developments and alpha launch details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Alpha">
    When alpha launches, your premium ring will automatically grant you access to the game.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shape the Future">
    Play, provide feedback, and help shape the future of Quack. Your participation in testnet will be rewarded based on specific metrics.
  </Step>
</Steps>
