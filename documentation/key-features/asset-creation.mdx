# Create Your Own Items

<Note>
  Coming soon: Design, create, and earn from your own in-game items through our innovative Blueprint system powered by TokenFabric.xyz!
</Note>

## The Blueprint System

Blueprints are special NFTs that contain the metadata and crafting specifications for in-game items. As a creator, you can design unique items that other players can craft and use in the Quack universe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Blueprint">
    Design your item on TokenFabric.xyz by defining:
    - Item appearance and metadata
    - Item category and specifications
    - Resource requirements for crafting
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Terms">
    Configure your Blueprint's economics:
    - Set blueprint purchase fees
    - Define crafting royalties
    - Establish marketplace royalties
  </Step>
  <Step title="List on TokenFabric">
    Make your Blueprint available on the TokenFabric marketplace for others to purchase and use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Continuously">
    Receive royalties from:
    - Blueprint sales
    - Item crafting
    - Secondary market trades
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Crafting System

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="hammer" title="Resource Requirements">
    Each item category has specific resource ranges. Your Blueprint's requirements must fall within these predetermined ranges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="recycle" title="Item Lifecycle">
    Items can be crafted, used, and potentially deconstructed, with craft limits recovering when items are burned or deconstructed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shop" title="Resource Market">
    Players can obtain crafting resources through gameplay or by trading with other players in the marketplace.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
