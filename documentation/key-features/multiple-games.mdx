# Your Quack Adventure Begins

<Note>
  Every great duck's journey begins with an egg. All eggs and ducks are NFTs that can be freely traded on our marketplace. Your digital assets are truly yours to own, trade, or sell!
</Note>

## Duck Lifecycle

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="egg" title="Duck Cycles">
    Each egg cycle features unique attribute ranges and special characteristics. Stay tuned to our announcements for new cycle releases and their special features!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="dna" title="Random Attributes">
    Your duck's attributes are a combination of cycle-specific ranges and completely random traits, ensuring each duck is unique.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shirt" title="Equipment & Customization">
    Each duck can wear various equipment pieces to modify their stats and abilities. Equip items in multiple slots including body, head, hands, feet, rings, and more to customize your duck's performance across all game modes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shop" title="Marketplace">
    Trade your eggs, ducks, and items freely on our marketplace. Every asset in Quack is player-owned and tradeable.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
